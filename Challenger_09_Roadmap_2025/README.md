# QUANTIA – Official Roadmap 2025

## **Phase 1 – Core Token (Completed)**

* Deployment of **QTA v1** on Arbitrum.
* Contract verification & metadata configuration.
* Integration with MetaMask and token explorers.
* Repository structure with challenges and documentation.

---

## **Phase 2 – Governance & Treasury (Q1 – Q2 2025)**

### **2.1 Multisig Treasury (Challenge_03)**

* Creation of a secure MultiSig Wallet for:

  * Team funds
  * Ecosystem treasury
  * Liquidity management
* Governance rules for approvals and spending.

### **2.2 Token Vesting System (Challenge_04)**

* Smart contract for progressive release of:

  * Team allocation
  * Advisors
  * Seed investors
  * Ecosystem grants
* Transparent schedule documented publicly.

### **2.3 Anti-Bot Module (Challenge_06)**

* Protection against:

  * Sniper bots
  * MEV attacks
  * Sandwich attacks
* Fair launch improvements for future liquidity events.

---

## **Phase 3 – Interoperability (Q2 2025)**

### **3.1 Chainlink Price Oracle Integration (Challenge_05)**

* Reading real-time price feeds.
* Compatibility with DEX aggregators and DeFi apps.

### **3.2 Future Bridge Feasibility Study**

* Research on bridging QTA to:

  * Base
  * BNB Chain
  * Polygon zkEVM
* Security analysis + cost evaluation.

---

## **Phase 4 – Scalability & Upgrades (Q3 – Q4 2025)**

### **4.1 QTA v2 Upgradeable Contract (Challenge_07)**

* Migration to UUPS or Transparent Proxy.
* Allow feature expansion without redeployment.
* Prepare foundation for quantum-resistant modules.

### **4.2 Quantum Security Research**

* Study post-quantum cryptography models.
* Evaluate zk-proof upgrades and state trees.
* Early prototype of quantum-safe hashing for QTA.

* Whitepaper v1
